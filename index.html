A La Pâquerette summer
Since the summer of 1989, we here at La Pâquerette have been bringing you the spirit of French fashion. Now, almost 33 years later, summer is almost here again! Enjoy the sunshines of southern France with a collections defined by bold colours & cool fabrics.
With our Spring/Summer 2022 collection, ease your mind and body into soft, supple pieces that transition seamlessly from innerwear to clothing.
Celebrating Pride 2022
To celebrate Pride all summer long, we’ve invited five creatives from five cities to show their work in five of our stores. Meet Yasha, Djamila, ShayShay, Stella, and Hanna, as they share their perspectives on re-envisioning the world through art, existence, and style.
Additionally, 5% of every sale throughout the month of June will be donated to OutRight Action International, a nonprofit that works at the international, regional, and national levels to research, document, defend, and advance human rights for LGBTQIA+ people around the world.

Women's Pre-Fall 2022
